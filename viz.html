USAGE:
  • each dot represents a casefile.
  • to zoom: scroll (PC) or pinch (mobile)
  • Hover on items in the legend to highlight matching casefiles.
  • Click on items in the legend to lock in highlighting.
  • Hovering on a point will show details and highlight similarly categorized points.
  • The "layout" buttons rotate between perspectives.
  • The "toggle images" button turns on image rendering. Images will load dynamically as you zoom.
